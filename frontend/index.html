The mathematics behind every bundle

Three Metrics.
Infinite Revenue.

Apriori distills millions of transactions into three precise signals — every recommendation shown to a shopper is statistically validated.

Support

Frequency Filter

Supp(A) = Freq(A) / N

Baseline popularity of any product in your catalog. Items below 5% support are trimmed before rule generation — eliminating noise.

62% avg coverage
Confidence

Purchase Probability

Conf(A→B) = Supp(A∪B) / Supp(A)

If a shopper buys A, what's the probability they also buy B? Only rules above 60% confidence surface to checkout.

71% peak confidence
Lift

Association Strength

Lift(A→B) = Conf(A→B) / Supp(B)

Lift ≥ 1.2 means buying A genuinely boosts purchase of B — not random chance. Filters out spurious correlations.

3.33× max lift

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before integrating SmartCart AI into your checkout workflow.

Apriori scans all transaction baskets to find frequent single items (support ≥ 5%), then iteratively builds pairs and triplets. The downward-closure principle ensures that if an itemset is infrequent, all of its supersets are discarded — trimming the search space exponentially. Final rules are filtered by lift ≥ 1.2 to confirm genuine co-purchase strength over random correlation.

Training Apriori on large datasets adds 2–15 seconds of CPU overhead per request. By pre-computing rules offline and serializing them to model.pkl, the Flask server loads the entire ruleset at boot. Each checkout query becomes a dictionary lookup — O(1) time complexity regardless of catalog size, with median response times under 0.4ms.
